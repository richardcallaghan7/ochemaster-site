Visit-Based Throw Diagnostics

OcheMaster doesn't chase textbook form. It identifies mechanical drift, release timing variability, and alignment against high-performing bands — so you can see what's breaking and why.

  • Stability Score - How consistent is your throw across a visit? Measures mechanical drift.
  • Alignment Score - How close are you to high-performing patterns? Not textbook — data-driven.
  • Drift Detection - See which throw in the visit broke from your baseline.
  • Session Tracking - Watch your stability and alignment evolve over time.
