The Stuff You Can't See

Every player has something in their throw they can't spot. Maybe your elbow drifts on dart 2. Maybe your release timing varies by half a second between throws. OcheIQ shows you what's actually happening.

  • Your Level - See where you stand: Developing, Advanced, or Elite. Track your progress session to session.
  • Timing Numbers - "Dart 2 was 0.65 seconds slower than the others" — not vague feelings, actual numbers.
  • Frame-by-Frame Replay - Step through each throw with a skeleton overlay. See exactly what your arm, elbow, and wrist do at every point — the stuff you can't catch in real time or slow-mo.
  • Find the Problem Dart - See exactly which throw broke from your pattern and why.
  • Patterns Across Sessions - Find what keeps going wrong — like dart 3 always rushing — and track whether it's improving.
  • Coaching - Your top issue, a specific drill to fix it, and coaching that remembers what you've worked on.

Why I Built This

I've got hours of slow-mo footage of myself throwing darts. Watched it back so many times. I could tell when something was off but I could never figure out what exactly.

Was my elbow moving? Timing different? No idea. Just me squinting at my phone at 11pm like "that one looked weird I think?"

So I built this. Basically the feedback I wished I could get without paying for a coach or having someone watch every session. Still a work in progress - if something's broken or you want a feature just email me.

- Richard

What's in the app now

Feedback-first results that tell you exactly what happened — which dart broke your pattern and what to work on. A throw corridor showing your consistency as a color-coded ribbon.
