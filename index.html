Visit-Based Throw Diagnostics

OcheMaster doesn't chase textbook form. It identifies mechanical drift, release timing variability, and alignment against high-performing bands — so you can see what's breaking and why.

  • Skill Bands - See where you stand: Developing, Advanced, or Elite. Track progress to the next level.
  • Concrete Timing - "Dart 2 was noticeably slower (0.15s)" — not vague percentages.
  • Standout Detection - See which throw in the visit broke from your pattern.
  • Pattern Tracking - Find recurring tendencies across sessions with tips to fix them.
