Why I Built This

I've got hours of slow-mo footage of myself throwing darts. Watched it back so many times. I could tell when something was off but I could never figure out what exactly.

Was my elbow moving? Timing different? No idea. Just me squinting at my phone at 11pm like "that one looked weird I think?"

So I built this. Basically the feedback I wished I could get without paying for a coach or having someone watch every session. Still a work in progress - if something's broken or you want a feature just email me.

- Richard
